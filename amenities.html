World-Class Amenities

Indulge in unparalleled relaxation and recreation.

The Serenity Spa

Melt away your stress in our award-winning holistic spa. Combining ancient healing traditions with modern therapeutic techniques, our expert therapists provide treatments designed to rejuvenate your mind, body, and spirit.

  • Deep tissue and hot stone massages
  • Rejuvenating organic facials
  • Eucalyptus steam room & dry sauna
  • Couples therapy suites

Infinity Edge Pool

Take a dip in our spectacular infinity pool that creates the illusion of merging directly into the ocean horizon. It is the perfect spot to soak up the sun or enjoy a refreshing cocktail without ever leaving the water.

  • Temperature-controlled water year-round
  • Swim-up bar serving tropical drinks
  • Private luxury cabanas available for rent
  • Dedicated kids' splash zone nearby

Private Beach Access

Step directly from the resort onto miles of pristine, white sandy beaches. Exclusively reserved for our guests, our beach offers a quiet sanctuary for morning strolls, sunset viewing, or thrilling water sports.

  • Complimentary sun loungers and umbrellas
  • Stand-up paddleboard and kayak rentals
  • Beachside food and beverage service
  • Guided snorkeling tours

State-of-the-Art Fitness Center

Maintain your wellness routine while traveling. Our 24-hour fitness center features floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the gardens, ensuring an inspiring workout environment equipped with the latest technology.

  • Peloton bikes and advanced treadmills
  • Comprehensive free-weight section
  • Complimentary morning beachfront yoga
  • Personal training sessions available
